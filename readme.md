# Retrominoes

Retrominoes is a stacking puzzle game. Players must manipulate randomly-generated [polyominoes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Polyomino) before they "fall" down the screen far enough to collide with the bottom or another polyomino.

## Controls

On your Retropad, push the directional buttons and the A, B, X, and Y buttons to move and rotate the polyomino as it falls toward the bottom of the screen.

__Left and Right:__ Slide the polyomino left or right across the field to line it up with a favorable position below.

__Down__: Holding this button increases the speed at which the polyomino descends.

__B__: Rotate the polyomino 90 degrees clockwise to find a favorable orientation.

__A__: Rotate the polyomino 90 degrees counter-clockwise.

__X__: Move the 2nd polyomino in the "NEXT" display to the front of the list.

__Y__: Move the _last_ polyomino in the "NEXT" display to the front of the list.
